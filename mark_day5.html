Day 5 – Light Legs & Arms (Neck‑Friendly)
← Back to overview

Duration: 60 minutes

Target muscle groups: Lower body and arms (light)

Exercise level: Light to moderate pace

Leg Extension (Light)
3×15 Controlled 45 s Machine
Use light weight; focus on full range of motion.
Leg Curls (Light)
3×15 Controlled 45 s Machine
Use light weight; contract hamstrings fully on each rep.
Alternating Bicep Curls
3×12/arm Controlled 45 s Dumbbells
Alternate arms; keep elbows close to your body; avoid swinging.
Tricep Pushdowns
3×12 Controlled 45 s Cable
Keep elbows close to your sides; press down through the rope/bar.
Preacher Curls (Machine)
3×12 Slow 45 s Machine
Rest arms fully on the pad; lower slowly for full stretch.
Tricep Extension (Crossing Cables)
3×12 Controlled 45 s Cables
Use cross cables; maintain stable elbows; extend fully.

Cardio

Stairmaster – 30 min at moderate intensity.
