How to read it (uniform rules)

  • Tempo = eccentric–pause–concentric (e.g., 3‑1‑3 = 3 s down, 1 s hold, 3 s up). Slow means going to maximum fatigue while controlling each repetition. Make sure you are engaging the muscles and activating the correct movers.
  • Do not explode through exercises!
  • Warm‑up block happens before every lift day. Pick one exercise.
  • If anything pinches, shorten the range of motion, lighten the load, or swap the listed alternative.
  • Superset: Doing one exercise followed immediately by another with no rest in between. This could be a push → pull combination; two different exercises paired back‑to‑back.
  • Dropset: Same exercise, lowering the weight after completing reps to failure with no rest.
