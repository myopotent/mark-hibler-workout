Day 1 – Lower/Core (Neck‑Friendly)
← Back to overview

Duration: 50–60 minutes

Target muscle groups: Legs (quads, hamstrings, glutes), calves & core

Exercise level: Slow to moderate pace

Leg Press
3×10 Slow 45 s Machine
Feet shoulder‑width; full, controlled depth.
Leg Extension Machine
3×8–10 Slow 45 s Machine
Knees aligned with shins; lighten weight.
Seated Leg Curl
3×10–12 Slow 45 s Machine
Squeeze hamstrings; slow return.
Bulgarian Split Squat
3×8/leg Controlled 45 s Dumbbells
Keep chest upright; knee behind toes.
Smith Machine Squats
3×12 Slow 60 s Smith Machine
Keep feet slightly forward, maintain neutral spine and descend to comfortable depth.
Single Leg Extension
3×10/leg Controlled 45 s Machine
Perform one leg at a time; focus on a controlled contraction and smooth lower.
Hip Thrust / Glute Bridge
3×12 Slow 45 s Machine/Barbell
Engage glutes; avoid arching lower back.
Standing Calf Raise
3×12–15 Slow 30 s Machine/Smith
Pause at top & bottom.
Hip Abduction/Adduction
2×12/side Slow 30 s Machine
Controlled range of motion.

Core

  • Plank – 2×30–60 s
  • Side Plank – 2×30 s per side
  • Crunches – 2×15

Cardio

Stairmaster – 15 min at moderate intensity.
