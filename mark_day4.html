Day 4 – Upper Push/Pull (Neck‑Friendly)
← Back to overview

Duration: 60 minutes

Target muscle groups: Upper body (chest, back, shoulders & arms)

Exercise level: Moderate pace

Cable Chest Fly
3×12 Controlled 45 s Machine
Keep elbows slightly bent; squeeze chest at peak.
↑ Superset ↓
Reverse Pec Deck
3×12 Controlled 45 s Machine
Focus on rear delts; avoid neck strain by keeping head neutral.
Chest Supported Row
3×12 Slow 45 s Machine
Support your chest on an incline bench; pull toward your torso and keep shoulders down and back.
↑ Superset ↓
Reverse Curls (EZ Bar)
3×12 Controlled 45 s EZ Bar
Palms facing down; use an EZ bar to curl up, then lower slowly.
Lat Pull Downs
3×12 Slow 45 s Machine/Cable
Do not pull the bar behind your head; keep your spine neutral.
↑ Superset ↓
Curls (Lat Pulldown Seat)
3×12 Controlled 45 s Machine
Use the lat pulldown seat for support; perform curls with controlled motion.
Tricep Extension (Rope)
3×12 Slow 45 s Cable/Rope
Use a rope attachment; keep elbows tucked and extend fully.
↑ Superset ↓
Hammer Curl
3×12 Controlled 45 s Dumbbells
Keep palms facing each other; avoid swinging.
Lateral Raises
3×10 Slow 45 s Dumbbells
Raise only to shoulder height; avoid shrugging.
Tricep Extension (Crossing Cables)
3×12 Controlled 45 s Cross Cables
Use crossing cables; keep elbows in and maintain a neutral neck.
↑ Superset ↓
Assisted Pull Ups
3×10 Slow 45 s Machine
Use an assisted pull‑up machine; keep your neck neutral and avoid swinging.
Preacher Curls
3×12 Slow 45 s EZ Bar/Bench
Ensure your arms are completely rested on the pad; curl with control.

Cardio

Stairmaster – 15 min at moderate intensity.
